Our game uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized advertising, including:

If you purchase the “Remove Ads” option, Google Play Billing processes the transaction securely. We do not store payment information.

Our game is not directed at children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Choices
